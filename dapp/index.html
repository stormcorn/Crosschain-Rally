Boxes and Tiers?

We will give a detailed explanation here.

With certain cars performing differently than others, the tier system helps to sort the cars by overall-ability.

Boxes have 3 levels. Since T1 cars are dedicated to Web2.0 onboarders, T2-T7 are featured in the mystery boxes. Silver boxes yield either a T2 or T3 car, Gold boxes yield either a T4 or T5 car, and Flame boces yield either a T6 or T7 car. The car that is recieved is random within the tier range.

Purchase cars by clicking the "Buy" button, and a random vehicle belonging to that level will be minted. Cars are paid for in native coin such as CRO or BNB.
